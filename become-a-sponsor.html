Partner with ICAIF '26 to connect with leading researchers, practitioners, and innovators at the intersection of AI and Finance.

Why Sponsor ICAIF '26?

The ACM International Conference on AI in Finance (ICAIF) is a premier forum for sharing research and innovation. Sponsoring ICAIF '26 in Milan offers your organization unique opportunities to:

  • Recruit top talent: Connect with world-class researchers and highly skilled practitioners in AI and financial services.
  • Enhance brand visibility: Showcase your organization's commitment to innovation and thought leadership to a targeted, global audience.
  • Network with leaders: Build relationships with key decision-makers from academia, industry, and regulatory agencies.
