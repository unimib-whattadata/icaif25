Areas of Interest

ICAIF '26 welcomes high-quality submissions demonstrating the application of AI techniques to real-world financial problems.

Generative AI & Foundation Models

Generative AI, simulation, synthetic data generation, and foundation models for financial decision-making.

AI Agents & Reinforcement Learning

AI agents, multi-agent systems, reinforcement learning, sequential decision-making, meta-learning, and transfer learning.

NLP & Multimodal Analysis

Natural language processing and multimodal financial data analysis including text, image, time series, and audio.

Trustworthy & Responsible AI

Explainability, ethics, fairness, security, privacy, AI governance, computational regulation, and compliance.

Risk Management & Fraud Detection

AI-driven risk management, fraud detection, robustness, uncertainty quantification, and model validation and calibration.

Trading & Asset Management

Trading strategies, asset pricing, robot-advising, investment recommendations, financial time series analysis, and factor models.

Credit & Customer Analytics

Customer behavior analysis, credit decisioning, fraud detection for credit cards and mortgages, and early detection of firm defaults.

Blockchain & DeFi

Blockchain technology, cryptocurrency, federated learning, and decentralized finance applications.

Financial Inclusion & ESG

AI-driven financial inclusion initiatives and Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) investing applications.
